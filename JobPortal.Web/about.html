About Joblisting

JobListing is a global website that gives access to a wide range of jobs across several categories, such as green jobs, freelance offers, and seasonal jobs. Users can upload their resumes on the platform and submit them to potential employers and recruiters for review. Employers and recruiters can easily be connected to potential employees via targeted job ads. Universities can use JobListing to efficiently manage their recruitment process.
